Ways to Donate



All monetary donations are used to care for the cats at The Cat's Meow. Funds are used for cat supplies such as food, litter, toys, medicine, vet bills, etc.

1) You may send a monetary donation through Paypal or Venmo.

2) Send our most-needed items via our Amazon Wish List.

3) Link your Kroger rewards card to their community rewards program and select The Cat's Meow as your non-profit organization.

4) Shop on smile.amazon.com and select The Cat's Meow as your non-profit organization - no fees, no extra cost.
